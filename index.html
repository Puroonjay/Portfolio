About Me

Hey, I'm Puroonjay—a technologist and aspiring game developer with a love for gaming that's been with me since my 12th grade. Back then, I was just another game enthusiast, jumping into different worlds, mastering mechanics, and experiencing the magic of storytelling. But over time, something changed—my focus shifted from just playing games to wanting to create them. I realized I didn’t just want to experience great games; I wanted to contribute to the gaming industry and build immersive experiences myself.
In 2024, I took my passion to the next level by pursuing a BTech at Parul University, diving deep into technology and innovation. By 2025, I started actively exploring game development—learning, experimenting, and shaping my creative vision.
With a strong foundation and an insatiable curiosity, I'm driven to push boundaries and craft games that truly resonate with players.
